What?

Subatomiblack is a hard techno event for everyone. Hardtechno is a subgenre of electronic dance music and is distinguished by faster tempo with 160 beats per minute or more. Here you get a rhythm of an metronome with 160 beats per minute (bpm) which is classical for hard techno compared to pop music which has around 100 bpm as you can get here. Do you feel the energy?

How?

One popular style of dancing to hard techno music is Hakken (Hakkûh) which involves small steps that quickly follow each other to the rhythm. Another form of typical dancing is jumpstyle which is similar to hakken in a way but just like the name is already clarifying with more time in the air. Remember that typical hard techno is >= 160 bpm so eventually don’t forget a towel!

Why?

There are different obviously positive effects while dancing to hardtechno. Typically the day after is connected with some aching - dancing is a sport! And because of the fact you do sport you have all the positive effects from it like being one with the environment which is kind of a meditative state because you really deep dive into the music. No worries others don't see you because the room is dark with short interruptions of light. If you have any things in life which do not bring you to your peak of feelings at the moment then hard techno is the right thing.
